What We Do

QIJI provides integrated engineering and consultancy services supporting the complete lifecycle of industrial assets. We combine multidisciplinary engineering expertise with regulatory excellence to help energy companies deliver projects safely, efficiently and in full compliance.

Engineering & Consultancy Built Around Operational Excellence

We support clients from project conception through operations, delivering engineering consultancy, regulatory compliance, owner's engineering and technical advisory services that improve operational readiness, reliability and long-term asset performance.

Our multidisciplinary teams work alongside operators, EPC contractors, shipyards and asset owners to provide practical engineering solutions tailored to each project's technical and regulatory requirements.

By combining international engineering expertise with strong regulatory knowledge, we help clients reduce project risks, accelerate approvals and maintain compliance throughout the entire asset lifecycle.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide engineering and consultancy services to energy companies worldwide, ensuring industrial assets achieve operational readiness, regulatory compliance, reliability and maximum uptime through practical engineering solutions and long-term partnerships.
